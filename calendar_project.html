Calendar Project

Project Description
Create a cover for a calendar for each month with a specific theme gathered by interviewing people.
Process
Step 1: Interview
Interviewing people to understand what they think of when asked about a specific month.
Step 2: Sketch
From the data gathered from the interview, I start sketching ideas for the cover.
Step 3: Digitalize
After I have finished ideating and have a specific design, I move on to either Procreate or Illustrator to digitalize the design.
Step 4: Refine
After digitalizing the design has been finished, I would give it a break on my progress and come back later on to refine and finalize. This is because I am able to see improvements far better after a short break on working on it.
